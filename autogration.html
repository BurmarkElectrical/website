Multi-zone Audio

Our multi-zone audio installations are designed for flexibility allowing each room to enjoy its own soundtrack—whether it’s streaming services or your personal vinyl collection.

Zones can operate independently or be grouped for perfectly synchronised whole-home audio. From compact, single-stereo speakers in smaller rooms to powerful systems capable of filling your home with party-level volume, we ensure the right speaker is chosen for every zone. The result is a seamless, immersive listening experience throughout.

Shading

Electrical blinds and curtains that adjust to time, temperature, and presence or simply from your wall switch. Automatically close your blinds to simulate presence while your away from home or just when the sun goes down.

External louvers or shades can be automatically stowed if the wind reaches pre-defined values.

Burmark advocates for wired shading solutions—because in a world full of devices to charge, your shades shouldn’t be one of them.

Climate - HVAC

Climate control with zoned heating and/or cooling.

Modern climate control systems can utilise discreet temperature sensors integrated into room switches, eliminating the need for traditional thermostats while maintaining precise, seamless comfort in the background.

building backbone - KNX

KNX is a trusted, smart building standard that connects all key functions—like lighting, HVAC, blinds, audio, access control and more into one seamless system.

Burmark opts for wired KNX systems which uses a 2 core cable because it offers unmatched reliability and flexibility of install.

With KNX, you can control your space using switches, apps, voice control, or fully automated scenes tailored to your lifestyle.

Supported by over 500 global manufacturers, KNX is a future-ready system that can grow and adapt with your needs, without the hassle of redoing wiring or replacing devices.
