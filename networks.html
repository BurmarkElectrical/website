Data Networks & Wireless

A well implemented data network connects all your devices and keeps your internet fast and stable. We design and install wired networks using quality structured cabling for maximum performance. Wireless access points are strategically placed to provide consistent coverage throughout your property, allowing you to roam freely without connection disruption.

If your current network has poor coverage or isn’t meeting your needs, we can upgrade or install a new system to improve your connectivity.

Security & CCTV

At Burmark we choose wired security systems to provide stable, uninterrupted security coverage by eliminating common wireless issues like interference and signal dropouts - keeping your property safe at all times.

Whether you seek a camera at your front entrance or a sophisticated network recording server, we deliver solutions that keep you in complete control — with local storage for privacy and peace of mind.

Starlink and VSAT

With a background in marine, we understand the challenges of communications in remote and mobile environments.

If you’re on a remote farm, off-grid property, or anywhere with limited or mobile connectivity, Burmark is able to assist with installation or consultation for satellite communications including Starlink and VSAT systems.
