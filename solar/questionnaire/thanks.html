thank you! we have received your solar questionnaire

whats next?

Based on your responses, we are preparing an initial assessment and indicative system size tailored to your requirements.

We will then be in contact for the next stage of the process:


  1. Conduct a physical site survey

    Our team will visit your property to verify roof conditions, orientation, shading, and any other factors critical to the system’s performance and integration.

  2. Finalise system design

    Using insights from the site survey, we will refine the solar system layout, selecting the optimal components and configuration tailored to your objectives and budget.

  3. Submit quote for your review

    Once the design is complete, we will provide a comprehensive proposal, including expected performance, costs and timelines, so you can make an informed decision.
