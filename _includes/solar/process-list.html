• 01

    Client consultation

    We begin by understanding your energy requirements, usage patterns and system goals — ensuring any solution we design is built around your needs from the outset.

  • 02

    Site survey

    A thorough on-site assessment covering electrical infrastructure, structural suitability, shading, orientation and any network or planning restrictions.

  • 03

    System design

    We engineer a system that works within the constraints of your site and budget — sized correctly, laid out precisely and specified for long-term performance.

  • 04

    Network application

    We handle the submission of your distributed generation application to the network operator, managing the process on your behalf from start to approval.

  • 05

    Installation and commissioning

    Our team schedules, installs and fully tests your system — leaving site only once everything is performing to specification.

  • 06

    Export meter installation

    Where export to the grid is required, we coordinate the request for an export meter to be installed by your network operator.

  • 07

    Ongoing maintenance and monitoring

    We don't walk away at handover. Ongoing monitoring and maintenance keeps your system running at peak efficiency for the long term.
